Lets suppose you want to add up all the money you spent in this month. Usually you would look at all the bills and add them up. This is boring work we could avoid. Lets imagine you had a genie, everytime you would buy something you give the bill to the genie. The genie then uses his magic to read some of the information in the bills and then does a couple of things.

The genie adds up all the bills so that you could know the total amount you spent.

The genie can see the dates in the bills and correctly tells you how much you spent in the current month.

The genie is also very smart and knows when we've spent money on food or bought a third bag.

The genie's magic lets him do these things in a fraction of a second, saving us a lot of time.
